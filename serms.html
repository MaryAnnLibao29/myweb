"BE STILL"

Sunday Service

November 8, 2020

Psalm 46:10

-Still means free from turbulence or commotion. Calmness or silence. At peace.


What situation are we to BE STILL?

1. When you feel like GOD IS NOT ANSWERING YOU PRAYERS!

(Jeremiah 29:12-13)

The plans of the Lord is always better than what you have in mind. He prepared greater things in our life but sometimes we can't understand.

He knows every detail in your life so stop doubting the Love of God.

God will never dissapoint you. God knows what your heart truly needs and he will give you the best in life and you will experience the greatness of your love.

When he says 'No' it simply means he is preparing something better for you. When he says 'Wait' he is preparing you for the blessings. Then you will realize that everything 'Worth it'.


2. When you GO THROUGH BROKENNESS!

(Psalm 147:3)

God can send a broken hearts. He can heal the wounds inside.

God can do impossible things. He can perform miracles in your life. He will restore your past.

You can't change it. But God turn it into powerful testimonies.

He just want us to grow and mature.

Brokenness can save you. He doesn't want to hurt you. He doesn't want to destroyer your life. His ways are just mysterious.

Learn to endure and keep your faith.


3. When you SUFFER SICKNESSES!

We suffer sicknesses because of self abuse.

Because of abundance we are having so much food as if there's no more tomorrow. And at the end we might regret.

God loves us. He is our great healer. Always ask God in prayer to heal you.

God corrects us when we suffer all these.

God heals inside. He heals our heart. He is the greatest surgeon.

What we need is the assurance that God is with you no matter what happens.

God is the best doctor.


4. When you are OUT OF STOCK!

(Eccleciastes 3:11, Phillipians 4:19)

When everything we need is run out, learn to endure and keep your faith.

God will supply all your needs according to his riches in glory by Christ Jesus.


  Turn your complaints into worship and you will feel so relieved inside. Make Him not only your priority but make God the center of your life and everything else will follow. Fot He has made everything beautiful in his time. He also set eternity to the human heart yet no one can do what God has done from beginning to end. So stop worrying, instead Lift up all your burdens to God and surrender everything to Him.


To God be the Glory! God Bless Us All!

"Why We ADORE GOD?"

Sunday Service

December 27, 2020

In the book of Ephesians

I. We adore him because of his AMAZING LOVE.

(Ephesians 5:2)

God exemplified his love to us when he gave his son for the forgiveness of our sins.

So we have to serve the Lord with all our might.

He sacrifice of his sons life for us. The ultimate love, unchangeable.


II. We adore God because of his DESCENT ATTITUDE.

(Romans 15:5)

Christlike attitude.

Watch out your attitude. God exemplify our attitude. Be humble.


III. We adore him because of his OVERFLOWING BLESSINGS!

(Ephesians 1:3)

It is in our life as Christians reflect God's goodness in our life.

It is overflowing blessings beyond our expectation.

Physical blessing!

Emotional blessing!

Spiritual blessings!

Jesus provide our needs!


IV. We adore God because he is our REFUGE IN TIMES OF DISTRESS!

God is our best healer, our protector.

In times of trouble God is always there. We always seek God's protection in times of trouble.

God will never leave us.

He wants us to be of people of faith. Nothing is impossible with God.

This gives us great confidence.


V. We adore God because EVERYTHING IS IN JESUS!

(Ephesians 2:10, Colossians 2:10)

I am nothing without Chirst.

Jesus is a total package in our life.

God give us everything.

All things are possible with God.

We are secured if we have Jesus in us.

Whatever we ask to God he will give it to you.

Make God as the center of your life.


To God be the Glory! God bless us all!
